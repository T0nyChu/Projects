  • June 1889 - Completed one of his most well-known paintings to this day, The Starry Sky.
  • 1890 - Most of his works were displayed and were praised by many critics
  • July 1890 - Was heavily infatuated with the countryside expressed through many of his last paintings; yet also depicted his extreme loneliness.
  • 27 July 1890 - Committed suicide by shooting himself in the chest. Passed away in his room in the Auberge Ravoux.
  • 30 July 1890 - Buried in the municipal cemetary of Auvers-sur-Oise.
